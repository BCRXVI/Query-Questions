Book Genres

There are many different genres of books but they all fall into one of 2 categories.

Fiction

All the books in this category aren't based off a true story.

Fiction books have much less variety than Non-Fiction.

Non-Fiction

All the books in the category are based off a true story.

Non-Fiction books have many different categories that are very diverse. Some of those categories are,

Science Fiction

Science Fiction is one of the most popular categories and it contains well known books like the Enders Game Series, The Martian, and others.

Fantasy

Fantasy contains probably the widest variety of books out of all the genres. It contains well known series such as the Fablehaven series, Five Kingdoms series, and the Beyonders series.
